\# VLAN Design



\## VLAN Overview



The infrastructure is segmented using dedicated VLANs.



| VLAN | Name | Purpose |

|------|------|----------|

| 10 | Users | Workstations |

| 20 | Servers | Infrastructure |



\---



\## Security Benefits



\- Network Isolation

\- Reduced Broadcast Domains

\- Easier Firewall Enforcement

\- Simplified Access Control



\---



\## Future Expansion



Additional VLANs can be introduced for:



\- Guest Access

\- Management

\- DMZ

\- IoT
